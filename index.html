about us

Alphanso Dine, a part of Cafe Day Global Limited, is Shillong’s favourite hangout for dinner and conversations. We strive to provide the best experience to our guests. Our dishes are prepared by the best chefs present in the city. We opened our restaurant in 2016 at Shillong – the youth and the young at heart immediately took to the restaurant, and it continues to be one of the most happening places in the city. Alphanso Dine to the youth is a “hangout” spot where they meet people, make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming dishes, so if your travel brings you to shillong, do stop by our outlets to get a taste from back home!
